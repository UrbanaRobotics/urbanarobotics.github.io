2021-22 Season Recap

June 22nd, 2022

When it was announced Team 8726 was not advancing to FIRST World Championships, it was effectively the end of our inaugural season as the CryptoHawks. As disappointed as the team was to not advance to the biggest robotics stage in the world as a rookie team, there was also a lot to celebrate and be proud of. When looking at the statistics and feedback from other veteran teams, 8726 was one of the most dominant and strongest rookie teams in recent history, stockpiling points and wins while we were still getting used to the whole thing
